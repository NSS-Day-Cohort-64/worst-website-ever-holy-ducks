We strive to establish a global network of rubber duck devotees who embrace the values of collaboration, creativity, and curiosity in their pursuit of programming excellence.

Our Core Values:

  • Rubber Duck Synergy: We believe that the presence of a rubber duck enhances our ability to think through code and uncover innovative solutions. Through individual reflection and collective exchange, we harness the power of rubber duck synergy to elevate our programming skills.

  • Community and Collaboration: We foster a strong sense of community where members support and uplift one another. We encourage collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and mentorship, empowering each member to thrive in their programming journey.
  • Ethical Coding: We uphold the highest standards of ethics and integrity in our coding practices. We prioritize transparency, security, and privacy, striving to create software that benefits society and respects individual rights.
  • Outreach and Impact: We seek to make a positive impact beyond our community. Through educational initiatives, outreach programs, and partnerships, we aim to inspire the next generation of rubber duck coders, promoting the joys and benefits of programming with a rubber duck by their side.
