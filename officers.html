Daddy Daffy

Meet Daffy, the quacky member of the esteemed Duck Cult. Daffy may look like your average duck, but he's no ordinary bird. He's dedicated his life to the teachings of the Duck Cult, which include the sacred art of duck yoga and the importance of a balanced diet of bread crumbs and algae. When he's not busy recruiting new members to the flock, you can find Daffy leading the congregation in spirited renditions of "Fly Like an Eagle" and "I Believe I Can Fly." Don't let his feathers fool you, Daffy is one cool duck!
